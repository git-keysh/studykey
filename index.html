What We're All About

Study Key generates proper study materials for CXC students using code that processes real past papers. We break down CSEC Paper 1 and Paper 2 exams into structured quizzes with instant feedback, so you spend less time searching and more time learning.

No PDFs to download. No answer keys to hunt for. Just pick a subject, pick a year, and start practicing.

  • Real CSEC English A Paper 1 questions from 2017–2024
  • Instant feedback on every answer
  • Track your scores and see what needs work
  • Works on desktop, tablet, and phone
